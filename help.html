How to play?

Controlls

This game requires access to mouse and keypad.

To move player on the screen use A, W, S, D buttons.

To shoot to the asteroid or any rocket aim the mouse and click the mouse left button to launch the missle.

Points

To win this games you have to shoot every alien asteroid on the screen an the rockets launched by asteroids that are going to destroy the player.

Game Over

Player has many lives, it depends on the dificulty mode of the players choice. When you the player loose the last life then the game is over and the playser can see the score.
